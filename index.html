Our Ideals

____________________________________________________________

I want to show women the beauty within each in every one of us. I want women to go back to the basics, the natural, and embrace themselves for who they really and truly are. Girls today are completely lost and caught up in this fabricated idea of beauty. I've finally had enough and I am putting an end to this "beauty" everyone strives so hard to achieve.

I want you to feel beautiful about you, and understand that everyone has their own unique version of what's beautiful and you shouldn't strive to meet societies standards of what is beautiful, only your own standards.

I am building a brand for women to feel like women. I am building a brand to encourage a movement, to encourage confidence. I am building a brand for the people.

I started doing nails, back when I was about 12 years old. Acrylic, Gel, designs, manicures and pedicures. I decided at the age of 21 to go back to school, get my certificates, my diploma, and create my own business. I attended and completed the Aesthetics program at St Clair College, and have over 16 years experience.
